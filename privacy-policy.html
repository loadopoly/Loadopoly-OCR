Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

GeoGraph Node ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using GeoGraph Node, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Document Content: Text, images, and data extracted from documents you scan using the app's OCR functionality.
  • User-Generated Data: Knowledge graphs, annotations, tags, and organizational structures you create within the app.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and display name.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect GPS coordinates and location metadata to geotag your scanned documents and artifacts. This is an optional feature that can be disabled.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Camera Access: We access your device's camera solely to capture images of documents for OCR processing. Images are processed locally or sent to our AI services for text extraction.

1.3 Third-Party Services

  • AI Processing: Document images and text may be processed using Google Gemini API for text extraction, analysis, and knowledge graph generation.
  • Blockchain Services (Optional): If you choose to use NFT minting features, transaction data may be recorded on public blockchain networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To perform OCR on your documents, build knowledge graphs, and provide the app's primary features.
  • Location Services: To associate geographic metadata with your scanned items when this feature is enabled.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
Data Licensing Note: Aggregated, anonymized datasets may be used for machine learning research and development. Individual user data is never sold or shared in identifiable form without explicit consent.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your documents and knowledge graphs are primarily stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Processing: When using AI features, data is transmitted securely to our processing servers and third-party AI services.
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit.
  • Retention: Processed data on our servers is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service, unless you request deletion.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered text extraction and analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure. Vercel Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable location services, camera access, or other permissions through your device settings.
  • Data Portability: Export your knowledge graphs and data in standard formats.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Children's Privacy

GeoGraph Node is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your jurisdiction. By using the app, you consent to such transfers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
